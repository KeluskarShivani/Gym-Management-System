About us

Fitness center Gym has been the authority on fitness since 2000. It was then that fitness legend Ankush Tarvankar took the knowledge and expertise he gained from training and opened up the first Gym in Mumbai, india. Opened long before the modern day health club existed, the original Gym featured homemade equipment and an unrivaled dedication to getting results. It was an instant hit. Early fitness enthusiasts flocked to the gym, and in 2000, Gold’s Gym received attention from public – a bodybuilding documentary starring Ankush Tarvankar . With its storied reputation of being "THE MUMBAISHRI" to go for the latest training techniques, and its legendary history of producing bodybuilding champions, for mumbai Today Fitness center Gym is the most recognized name in fitness serving more than 15 years around the mumbai. Always at the forefront of the fitness revolution, Fitness center Gym has continually evolved its profile by equipping gyms with the best amenities and the latest in cardio and strength training equipment as well as the most dynamic group exercise programs including kick boxing, group cycling, mixed martial arts, muscle endurance training, and gym fitness. At Fitness center, you’ll find an energetic, supportive environment full of all kinds of people who are committed to achieving their goals. Today more than ever, Fitness center Gym realizes that strength comes in many forms…whether it’s the strength to be a better parent, the strength to overcome diabetes or the strength to be a better athlete. At Fitness center, our goal is simple: We’re here to help you Know Your Own Strength®
